News Letter


The 15th Njavallil Day was celebrated with splendour and pomp at Njavallil Koonthanam House, Karimkunnam on 26th January, 2012. Dr.Johny Joseph Pazhayidam M.D, D.M , the Chief Cardiologist, Carithas Hospital was the chief guest for the event. His better half Mrs. Rani Johny also accompanied him.


It was indeed a happy family gathering â€“ with members even from distant hillocks in Idukki, Malabar, Pala, Kanjirapally and Manimala. The executive committee published a special supplement in newspaper, wherein we described the historic background of our family which begins as far back as from the 1st century onwards.


In the public meeting the president made a fervent appeal to the family members for the formation of a family welfare fund with a view to uplift the less fortunate members of our family. It is a matter of great satisfaction to let you know that we could reconstruct two houses and hand them over to the owners of the building who belong to our family. The committee would like to know how you react upon this suggestion for the formation of our family welfare fund.


On this auspicious occasion, the official website of our family was released by Rev. Dr. Thomas Kadankavil, which was sponsored and designed by Mr. Biju Koonthanam and Sibu Koonthanam respectively.


Besides, the annual day meeting re-elected the committee members who were scheduled to retire this year. The details of the all the committee members are available at http://www.njavallil.com/pages/committee.html. All the photos taken during the event can be viewed at http://www.njavallil.com/pages/photogallery.html


The convener of the meeting Mr. Antony Theruvankunnel, did a very good job in organizing the event flawlessly.


We would like to take this opportunity to extend heartfelt thanks to all the members who made the 15th Njavallil Day a grand success.
